Version 1.2.1

  • Security: Fixed possible technique malicious sites could use to bypass the extension.

  • Feature: Warning page shows more information than the banner. Including highlighting the exact unicode characters in the domain.
  • Feature: Added ability to whitelist specific characters in the options.
  • Feature: Options page now shows the unicode versions of whitelisted domains in addition to punycode.
  • Security: Changed method of remembering temporarily dismissed domains to a way that cannot be manipulated by the site.
