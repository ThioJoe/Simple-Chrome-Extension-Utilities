Why am I seeing this?
You're seeing this because you have the Unicode Domain Warning browser extension installed.

This extension alerts you when a website uses special Unicode characters in its domain name, which are used in some international domain languages, but can also be used in phishing attacks. This extension simply gives you a heads up in case such a domain was not expected.
